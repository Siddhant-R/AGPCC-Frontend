ABOUT OUR CLUB

AGGIE GRADUATE AND PROFESSIONAL COMMUNITY CLUB

OUR MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission at Aggie Graduate and Professional Community Club (AGPCC) is to connect graduate and professional students in an informal, relaxed environment. To stimulate conversation, friendships and professional connections within the Graduate Aggie Family.

Kristen K. Hicks

President

AGPCC

  • All graduate and professional students are invited!

    Our club is dedicated to our mission! Our goal is to introduce you to other graduate and professional students, outside of your department and lab. We host events all around the Bryan-College Station area. All events are open to ALL graduate and professional students at Texas A&M University.
