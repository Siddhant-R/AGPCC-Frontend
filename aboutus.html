ABOUT OUR CLUB

AGGIE GRADUATE AND PROFESSIONAL COMMUNITY CLUB



  • Aggie Graduate and Professional Community Club was formed due to a dream that was envisioned and inspired by the Community of Scholars event in Spring 2015. The founder and current president, Ms. Kristen Hicks, envisioned an organization that connected the Graduate Aggie Family.

    We at AGPCC are dedicated to increasing your Aggie personal and professional network by providing exclusive opportunities to stimulate conversations and connect with other graduate and professional students attending Texas A&M University. Furthermore, we are engaged in our community through several community service events across the Bryan College Station area. Our hopes as an organization is to create stronger bonds within the Graduate Aggie Family.

    Our leadership devotes our time and energy to ensure that we meet the needs of graduate and professional students, please provide us with feedback anytime. Any members can post photos or testimonials of their experiences with AGPCC at anytime on: https://www.facebook.com/agpcctamu
