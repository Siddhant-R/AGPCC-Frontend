Kristen Hicks

Kristen Hicks is the Founder and President of Aggie Graduate and Professional Community Club. She is from Arizona where she completed her Bachelors degree, played collegiate soccer and was very involved on campus. She is a third year doctoral student in the Department of Nutrition and Food Science. Her research interests are to improve education opportunities for physicians in Texas. She is also a Registered Dietitian and practices as a corporate dietitian. She is very involved in many professional associations as well as Young Professionals of Aggieland. Kristen is passionate about AGPCC and has plans to have it be a huge success.
